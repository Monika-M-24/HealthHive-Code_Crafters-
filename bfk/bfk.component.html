Knows how to use basic items in a first aid kit

Gloves should always be used for protection when providing first aid to someone else

First Aid Kit

  • Antiseptic cream or ointment: helps disinfect the infection to prevent bacterial growth
  • Band-Aids: to stop bleeding
  • Rubbing alcohol: disinfectant to kill bacteria
  • Scissors: used to cut adhesive tape
  • White tape: adhesive used to keep gauge pads in place
  • Cotton balls: used to blot the blood
  • Gloves: worn for protection from germs and bodily fluids
  • Mask: protect self from breathing in germs
